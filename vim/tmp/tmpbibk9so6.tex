The operation $\oplus$ is commutative and associative. Let $v$ be the number \[v=\frac{\sqrt[7]{17}-1}{\sqrt[7]{17}+1}.\]
What is the value of \[\underbrace{v\oplus v\oplus\cdots\oplus v}_{13\ \oplus\text{'s}}?\]
