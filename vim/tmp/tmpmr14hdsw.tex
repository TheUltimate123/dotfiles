A three-digit positive integer $\underline{abc}$ is $\emph{wavy}$ if $a<b>c$. How many three-digit positive integers are wavy?

---

By casework on $b$, the answer is $1\cdot2+2\cdot3+\cdots+8\cdot9=240$.
